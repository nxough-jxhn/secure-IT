# Secure-IT Namecard Art Prompts

**Format:** PNG, landscape rectangle ratio **`21:9`** (e.g. 1050×450 px or 2100×900 px for high-res)  
**Style reference:** Genshin Impact namecard aesthetic — lush, painterly, no text, no UI, pure background illustration  
**General rule:** All designs must be background-only with no text, names, logos, or UI elements. The left ~40% of the card should be relatively open/empty to allow overlaid text later.
